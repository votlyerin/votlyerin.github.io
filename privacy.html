Votly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized "Tastemaker" experience, we collect information that helps our algorithm understand your preferences:

Account Data: When you sign up via Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
Demographic Profile: To categorize your votes, we ask for your Age Range, General Location (Postal Code), Gender Identity, and Household Income Tier.
Interaction Data: We record the "PickIts" you vote on and the "Vibes" (e.g., Techie, Fashionista) you select during onboarding.

2. How We Use Your Data

Personalization: To show you dilemmas that match your specific interests.
Gamification: To track your "Accuracy Score" and manage your "Credits."
Market Research: We analyze trends to understand consumer preferences.

3. Data Sharing & Anonymization

Anonymized Aggregation: We may share aggregated, anonymized insights with brand partners (e.g., "70% of Tech Enthusiasts in the 25-34 age bracket preferred Option A").
No Personal Sale: We do not sell your personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name or email, to third parties.

4. Safety & Content Moderation

Third-Party Processing: We use automated tools to scan images for inappropriate content to keep our community safe.
reCAPTCHA: We use Google reCAPTCHA to prevent bots and ensure all votes come from real people.

5. Your Rights

Access & Correction: You can update your profile and preferences at any time in the app settings.
Right to Deletion: You may permanently delete your account and all associated data via the Delete Account button in your profile.
